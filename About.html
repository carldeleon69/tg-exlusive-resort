ABOUT US

TG Exclusive Resort & Events Place, "Your ultimate destination for relaxation and unforgettable events". Located at Barangay Masuso 3014 Pandi, Bulacan is an exquisite destination nestled in serene surroundings, designed to provide a luxurious escape and a perfect venue for unforgettable events. Our resort blends contemporary architecture with nature's beauty, offering an idyllic retreat for relaxation, celebrations, and gatherings.

MISSION

Our mission at TG Private Resort & Events Place is to deliver an unparalleled experience that exceeds our guests' expectations. We are committed to providing exceptional service, luxurious accommodations, and versatile event spaces, all while fostering a deep connection with nature. Our goal is to create memorable experiences that our guests will cherish for a lifetime.

VISION

Our vision is to be recognized as the premier destination for luxury and exclusivity in the resort and events industry. We aim to set the standard for excellence by continually enhancing our facilities and services, embracing sustainable practices, and creating a harmonious balance between luxury and nature. We aspire to be the top choice for guests seeking relaxation, celebration, and inspiration in an elegant and serene environment.

The Owner




Owner: PG Carpio




PG Carpio is a notable businessman and the owner of a premier resort and events venue in Bulacan, Philippines. Known for his entrepreneurial spirit and dedication to excellence, Carpio has successfully transformed his resort into a sought-after destination for exclusive events and luxurious getaways.
